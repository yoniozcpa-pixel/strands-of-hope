Strands of Hope - Logo Concepts

Designed for a UK charity supporting at-risk youth in Israel.
These concepts combine elements of weaving (strands), growth/youth, and connection.

1. The Growing Strands

Three strands weave together to form a strong trunk, branching out into leaves/sparks. Represents youth growth and flourishing through support.

2. The Embrace

Two outer strands form a protective shield or hands wrapping around a bright inner core (the youth). Symbolizes care, safety, and the UK-Israel connection.

3. The Woven Star

A subtle nod to Israel (Star of David) created by overlapping strands, bound together by a central circle representing unity and the continuous cycle of hope.

4. The Bridge of Hope

Strands forming a strong arch or bridge, representing the connection between the UK and Israel. The rising sun beneath the arch symbolizes a new dawn for the youth.
