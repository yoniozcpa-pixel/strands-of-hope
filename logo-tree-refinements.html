The Growing Strands

Refining the first concept: Three strands weaving together to form a strong trunk, branching out into leaves/sparks.

A. The Original

Clean, minimal, and abstract. The strands merge at the center and branch out symmetrically.

B. Woven Trunk & Leaves

The strands cross over each other more dynamically in the trunk, and the "sparks" are shaped more like leaves.

C. The Canopy

The strands merge tightly at the base and then spread out wide like a protective canopy, with more "sparks" of hope.
